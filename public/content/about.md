I am a researcher, engineer, and creator at the intersection of **AI Music**, **Musical Acoustics**, and **Human-Computer Interaction**.

At [Rhythm Culture Corporation](https://rhythmculture.com), I lead engineering on AI-powered music education tools and agent-based music production systems. Our work was recognized at the **NeurIPS Creative AI Track 2024**.

I completed my M.S. at National Taiwan University (advised by Prof. Lung-Pan Cheng), where I developed [FlueBricks](/projects/fluebricks) — a modular construction kit for designing customizable flute-like instruments through digital fabrication. The paper was accepted at **CHI 2026** (25% acceptance rate) and received the **Best Paper Award at TAICHI 2025**.

Previously, I worked at the Creative AI Lab at **Sony Group Corporation** in Tokyo, where I developed [DJTransGAN](/projects/djtransgan) — the first AI model for generating smooth DJ-like transitions between audio tracks. I also spent four years at **Academia Sinica's Music and AI Lab** (advised by Prof. Yi-Hsuan Yang), publishing 5+ papers at top MIR venues including ISMIR and ICASSP.
