A Taipei Music Center-funded beat-making tool for novices, aligning musicians, designers, and engineers on DSP integration.

## Project Goals

Create an accessible entry point for music production that:
- Requires zero prior musical training
- Runs entirely in the browser (no installation)
- Teaches fundamental rhythm and arrangement concepts
- Feels playful and exploratory

## Technical Stack

- **React** for UI components
- **Web Audio API** for real-time synthesis
- **Custom DSP chains** for effects processing
- **Tone.js** for timing and sequencing

## Design Philosophy

The interface prioritizes **visual feedback** and **immediate gratification**. Users can:
- Drop samples onto a grid
- Apply effects with visual knobs
- Hear changes instantly
- Export their creations

## Team Collaboration

As Tech Lead, I coordinated between:
- **Musicians** defining the creative workflow
- **Designers** crafting the visual language
- **Engineers** implementing performant audio pipelines

## Funding & Recognition

Supported by Taipei Music Center as part of their initiative to make music education more accessible across Taiwan.
